Why is IJSE the only Choice for IT, Computer Science and Software Engineering?

Suranga Inspired by its mission “assisting the students to be qualified professionally and producing individuals desired and demanded by the market standards of the existing setup”. IJSE, being a leading IT education provider in Sri Lanka has produced over 4300 skillful IT professionals occupying eminent positions in many reputed companies in the island. Since its inception in 2006, it focuses on enhancing the skills and attitudes which are desperately sought by the IT industry. Obtaining a software engineering degree from IJSE opens doors to countless possibilities in the industry both in Sri Lanka and abroad therefore IJSE is surely the best institute for after A/L course programs in IT and Computer Science. Distinguished as the only Oracle authorized training center in Sri Lanka, IJSE is undoubtedly the best choice for building a successful career in software engineering industry.

10th Anniversary

In last 10 years had produced 4200 software engineers to the nation....!

IJSE was started on the 10th January of 2006 in Panadura, opening doors for the younger generation to materialize their dream of becoming a software engineering / quality assurance Engineer through providing recognized qualifications in IT and Software Engineering. At the start IJSE marched forward with a highly dedicated team of ten individuals whose hard work resulted in opening the second branch in Galle. Within this short period of time, IJSE has achieved a leading position among the providers of Software Engineering degrees in Sri Lanka. The uniqueness of realizing the demanded programmers and IJSE’s infinite flexibilities to explore new ways and best products could mark the journey a fast moving result oriented one. This achievement in short has made our degree in software engineering highly desired and sought after out of all other IT and Computer Science degrees i n Sri Lanka. The Committed individuals of our team and student centered values have brought IJSE from 30 to 700 students following our various courses in IT and Software Engineering. Our records have indicated that we had produced more than 4200 valuable software engineering professionals to the nation, and as a Sri Lankan IT education Institute we are proud to say that, many students of our first batch were recruited by most prestigious software companies in Sri Lanka placing us above all other IT course providers from the very beginning of our journey as an institute for higher education in software engineering and IT/Computer Science.
